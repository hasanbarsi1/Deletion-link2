How to Delete Your Account

To permanently delete your Genesis: Magic AI Image account:

  1. Open the Genesis: Magic AI Image app on your device
  2. Navigate to Settings
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your decision
⚠️ Warning: This action is permanent. All your data, generated images, and account information will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account credentials
  • All generated images
  • User preferences and settings
  • Any subscription or purchase history
